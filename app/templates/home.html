         Our Purpose

Our cuisine classifier can identify the origin of cuisine by its ingredients! In addition, anyone looking for recipes can input ingredients they have into the classifier, and a recipe will pop up! It is handy for those who want to learn about where a cuisine originates from, and anyone looking for something new to cook can use our AI for a new recipe!
