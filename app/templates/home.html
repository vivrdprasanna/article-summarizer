About Our Product

By using multiple datasets, this product accurately distinguishes text messages based on importance and common text language to improve a form of communication where it is difficult to determine what texts are useful and which aren't.

Motives and Project Intents

Since the number of people who own cell phones increase day by day, texting is very prominent in this day and age.

No Scams, Just Safety

The Internet, while being convenient can be very dangerous. Thus, with this product, you can avoid getting yourself scammed out of thousands of dollars and keep your private personal info to yourself.

Spam vs Important Texts

When we receive texts, many don't know whether the texts we get are just spam, or they are actually important. Thus, this product will conveniently distinguish between spam or important texts.

Our Targets

Wanting to apply AI to real life, our targets are those who uses phones to text. In addition, this product assists those who are not accustomed to using technology to communicate.
