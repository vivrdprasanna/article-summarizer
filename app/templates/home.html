Executing the rest of our code

Then we split our data into training and test data. We import a tokenizer and get rid of words that fall below and threshold frequency and use the LSTM to execute the rest of our project.

LSTM

  • LSTM stands for Long Short Term Memory
  • We used LSTM because RNN, or Recurring Neural Network, has something called the vanishing gradient problem.
  • Essentially, because of the way an RNN system works, it has a very short-term memory, which means it works poorly with long sentences.
  • LSTM, on the other hand, has gates that regulate information, and even chooses to forget things.
